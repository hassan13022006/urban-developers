About Us

This biography of A.P.J. Abdul Kalam, former President of India, for Children is written by an eminent historian, Vice-Chancellor. Kalam as a scientist and technocrat elevated the position of India among the world of nations due to his multifaceted research findings from Satellite to nuclear experiments. He was a great humanist who dreamed the welfare of humanity. He taught the Indian youth to dream on their destiny along with the progress of this country. His is a thrilling life story of ups and downs but inspiring one for generations and generations of India. Dr. KK.N. Kurup narrated this story of Kalam for children everywhere and kindles a new light in their mind.
